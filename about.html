About

The person
behind the page.

Hi, I'm Nova — I write the kind of fantasy where nobody gets to stay clean.

I've always been drawn to stories that don't flinch. Trauma that actually shapes a character instead of disappearing by chapter three. Villains whose logic you can follow a little too easily. Heroines who survive not because they're the strongest in the room, but because they're the most cunning — the ones who were underestimated right up until it cost someone everything.

That's the kind of fantasy I write, and it's the kind I'm building right now with The Scales — a world bound by dragon-blood, sixteen soul-colours, and seven power classes, where the cost of a wrong choice doesn't end when you die. It just starts the next loop.

Off the page, my life is much quieter: I live with two cats who believe they co-author everything I write. One is a grey British Shorthair named Bobo, who supervises with the seriousness of a editor. The other, Himi, has the kind of unsettling blue eyes that probably belong in one of my books eventually. If you ever spot a cat tucked into one of my stories — that's not an accident.

I write for readers who want their fantasy emotionally honest: messy, sharp, a little dangerous, and never tied up too neatly. If that's you, you're exactly who I'm writing for.
