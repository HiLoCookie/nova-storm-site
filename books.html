Dragon-blood Fantasy · Book One

The Scales

Sixteen soul-colours. Seven powers. One world where dying is never the end of your problems.

In a world bound by dragon-blood, every soul is born marked by one of sixteen colours — and that colour decides everything: your power, your class, your place, and how far you're allowed to rise before the world decides you've risen too far.

When the rules of survival are written by something older and crueler than any kingdom, the only way through is to be smarter than the system built to contain you. This is a story about power that costs more than it gives, choices with no clean side, and the kind of woman who learns to use a rigged game against the people who rigged it.
