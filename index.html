About the Author

I write the people
polite stories leave out.

I'm Nova Storm — I write fantasy for readers who want their heroines sharp-witted and their villains a little too understandable. My stories live in the space where trauma doesn't get a clean resolution, morally gray means actually gray, and the smartest person in the room is usually the woman everyone underestimated.

When I'm not building worlds, I'm at my desk with my two cats supervising — and yes, there's always a cat hiding somewhere in the story too.
